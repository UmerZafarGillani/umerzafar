Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HeyDoctor telehealth platform

How secure is HeyDoctor for patient data?

HeyDoctor employs enterprise-grade security with end-to-end encryption for all video calls, data transmission, and storage. We are fully HIPAA compliant and regularly undergo independent security audits. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols.

What does the free trial include?

Our 30-day free trial includes full access to all premium features, unlimited consultations, all integrations, and dedicated onboarding support. No credit card is required to start, and you can cancel anytime during the trial period.

Can I integrate HeyDoctor with my existing EHR system?

Yes! HeyDoctor integrates with all major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, NextGen, and many others. Our API-first architecture allows for custom integrations with any healthcare software. Contact our team for specific integration requirements.

How does billing and payments work?

HeyDoctor integrates with Stripe for secure payment processing. You can set up automated invoicing, accept payments in multiple currencies, and manage subscriptions. Patients can pay directly through the platform, and all transactions are PCI compliant.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 technical support with dedicated account managers for enterprise clients. Our support includes phone, email, and live chat assistance, plus comprehensive documentation and video tutorials.

Can patients book appointments internationally?

Yes, HeyDoctor supports international telemedicine. We comply with global healthcare regulations including GDPR in Europe and local regulations worldwide. Patients can book appointments across time zones, and we support multiple languages and currencies.

How do automated prescriptions work?

Our ScriptPad integration automatically generates prescriptions during consultations. Doctors can select medications from a comprehensive database, and prescriptions are securely delivered to patients and pharmacies electronically. This eliminates manual paperwork and reduces errors.

What happens if there's a technical issue during a consultation?

Our platform includes automatic failover systems and backup communication channels. If video fails, consultations can continue via phone or chat. We maintain 99.9% uptime and have dedicated technical support available during all consultation hours.

Can I customize the platform for my clinic's needs?

Absolutely! HeyDoctor offers extensive customization options including branding, workflows, templates, and custom integrations. Enterprise clients get white-label solutions and dedicated development support for specific requirements.

How long does implementation take?

Most clinics are up and running within 1-2 weeks. Our dedicated onboarding team handles data migration, staff training, and integration setup. Enterprise implementations may take 4-6 weeks depending on complexity.
